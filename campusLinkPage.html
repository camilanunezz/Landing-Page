Project Description

CampusLink: UX Design

This project spanned across 3 months in a team of 4. We worked through phases to focus on studying human computing iteraction, primarily through the process of learning about user experience more than UI appearance. To do so, the phases consisted of analysis, design, prototype and evaluation. This project does not include an implementation of the application but rather a prototype of the user-interface. Most of the work went into identifying a problem, creating a product to solve the issue, and finding a client and users to work alongside. Our team conducted research gathering to gain insights on our problem and how to best implement a solution towards it by developing user requirements. The design process included creating storyboards and wireframes we later interpreted to create our prototype.This project was heavily focused on well-documentation, streamline of communication with our client, and intenstive teamwork.
